Earn 2% cashback on everyday in-store spending with HSBC's free debit card 💳

No annual fee. No fuss.

✅ Apply for the HSBC Global Everyday Debit Card (free to open online)
✅ Tap and go for in-person purchases under $100
✅ Use it for transport, groceries, cafés — community members report $10–20/month back
✅ Max out at $2,500/month eligible spend = up to $50/month (~$600/year) 💰

⚠️ Card doesn't work online — keep a separate card for e-commerce.
⚠️ 2% only applies per transaction under $100.

Mechanics and prices change anytime; check HSBC's current product terms before applying.
